About

  • Photo: Lida Bondarenko

    Design: Aksana Zinchanka

    Maxim Sarychau (b. 1987) is a visual artist working with photography, photojournalist and software developer from Minsk, Belarus. Currently based in Wroclaw, Poland.

    He works on long-term visual projects on the edge of photography, journalism and art. Maxim deals with the topics of violence of various forms and grades, both from authoritarian regimes and within traditional society. His main interest lies in political and human dimensions of collective memory and history.

    Maxim balances art activities with developing software and gardening.

    He is available for editorial photography assignments in Poland.
